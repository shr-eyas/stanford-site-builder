# Open Positions

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our lab!

## PhD Positions

We are currently recruiting PhD students to start in Fall 2025. Ideal candidates have:

- Strong background in computer science, robotics, or related fields
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, JAX)
- Familiarity with robotics systems and ROS
- Published research is a plus but not required

**To apply:** Please apply through the [Stanford CS PhD program](https://cs.stanford.edu/admissions/phd). Mention the REAL lab in your application materials.

## Postdoctoral Positions

We have openings for postdoctoral researchers in the following areas:

- Robot learning and manipulation
- Foundation models for robotics
- Human-robot interaction

**Requirements:**
- PhD in robotics, computer science, or related field
- Strong publication record
- Experience with real robot systems

**To apply:** Send your CV, research statement, and three representative papers to [real-lab@stanford.edu](mailto:real-lab@stanford.edu)

## Visiting Researchers

We welcome visiting PhD students and researchers for collaborative projects. Visits are typically 3-12 months.

**To apply:** Email us with your CV and a brief description of your research interests and proposed collaboration.

## Undergraduate Research

Stanford undergraduates interested in robotics research are encouraged to reach out. We offer research opportunities during the academic year and summer.

**To apply:** Email us with your CV, transcript, and research interests.
